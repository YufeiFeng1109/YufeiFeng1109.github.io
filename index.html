About

Yufei Feng received his B.S. in Computer Engineering from Michigan State University in 2022 and his M.S. in Computer Engineering from Northeastern University in 2024. He is currently a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Northeastern University. His research interests are broadly in experimental wireless networking with a focus on multi-faceted characterization of operational 5G networks.
